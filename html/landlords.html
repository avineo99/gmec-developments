Stress-Free Property Management for Landlords

Guaranteed Rent & Hassle-Free Solutions

A Landlord today is tough, especially if property is personally owned. With constant legislative and tax changes, rising costs, and increased responsibilities, paperwork many landlords are questioning whether it’s worth staying in the market. Some are choosing to exit entirely, while others still want the rental income but without the day-to-day hassles.

At GMEC Developments and Lettings, we offer Guaranteed Rent on long term contracts of up to 6 years, ensuring you as the landlord receive a fixed monthly income, whilst we look after your property. We will invest OUR MONEY into your property so its Hotel Quality Standard and keep you updated with pictures of its pristine condition Whether you’re looking for a hassle-free way to stay in the market or need a bespoke solution to sell, we can help.

The Challenges you daily Face

  • Government Regulations – Constantly changing rules that make renting more complex and expensive.
  • Rising Costs – Higher mortgage rates, maintenance expenses, and compliance costs eating into profits.
  • Tenant Issues – Late payments, property damage, and legal headaches.
  • Void Periods – Unoccupied properties mean lost income and ongoing bills.
  • Time & Stress – Managing a rental property takes time, effort, and patience, wouldn’t you prefer to relax or spend the time travelling, sitting on the beach with the income.

If you’re feeling overwhelmed, you’re not alone. Many landlords are reconsidering their options, and that’s where we can help de stress you.

Did you know that we also offer Guaranteed Rent to HMO landlords? If you’re tired of managing a house full of tenants or struggling to fill your rooms, we can help. Let us take the hassle off your hands and bring your HMO back to life.

What You Get with Guaranteed Rent

With our Guaranteed Rent scheme, you can enjoy a reliable, hands-off approach to property management while still benefiting from a stable rental income.

  • Guaranteed Rent Every Month – No delays, no missed payments.
  • No Void Periods – You’ll receive rent even when the property is unoccupied.
  • No Tenants to Manage – We handle all guest / tenant interactions
  • No Chasing Payments – Your income is secure and consistent.
  • No Marketing Rooms or Finding New Tenants – We take care of occupancy.
  • No Day-to-Day Maintenance – We conduct regular inspections and handle minor upkeep, so you don’t have to worry about leaking taps or clogged toilets.
  • No Cleaning – Our professional cleaners maintain the property.
  • No Decorating Costs – We take care of painting, decorating, and furnishing where necessary.
  • No Utility Bills or Council Tax – We cover these expenses.
  • Less Hassle, More Peace of Mind!

Unlike traditional letting, our model ensures your property is cared for while providing you with complete financial security and proof to maintain your confidence its still in great condition month after month, year after year.
