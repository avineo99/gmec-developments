Corporate Lets

High-Quality Accommodation for Your Workforce.

Finding the right accommodation for your team, subcontractors, direct employees can be a challenge. Expensive hotel stays, limited availability to cater for your whole team, just rooms no private work space or relaxing space and a lack of flexibility can make short- or long-term placements more difficult than they need to be.

GMEC developments and Lettings, we offer fully serviced accommodation for corporate clients, providing a cost-effective and comfortable solution for housing your workforce. Whether you need a few rooms for a short-term project or accommodation for a larger team on a six-month contract, we can help.

Why Choose Serviced Accommodation?

  • Cost-Effective – Avoid expensive hotel bills with just single rooms no private facilities for groups or teams
  • Comfortable & Fully Equipped – Fully equipped, Large Screen TV with HDMI cables, Coffee Pod Machines, Full equipped kitchens for the whole team to be together.
  • Clean and Managed – Our properties are regularly inspected, well- maintained, and come with cleaning services included.
  • Flexible Terms – Whether you need accommodation for a few weeks or several months, we can tailor our services to suit your business needs, saving on moving, time and booking fees.
  • Better for Employee Wellbeing  – Your staff will have access to fully furnished homes, offering a more comfortable and relaxed environment compared to a hotel stay.
  • Strategic Workforce Deployment – Place your best employees where they are needed most, without the hassle of hiring locally.

The Right Accommodation for Your Business

We work closely with businesses of all sizes, offering a hassle-free solution to corporate housing needs. Our properties are Hotel Standard move-in ready and designed to provide a home-away-from-home experience for your workforce.

Whether you’re housing contractors, project teams, or relocating staff short term , we ensure they are well accommodated so they can focus on the job at hand.
