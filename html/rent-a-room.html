RENT A ROOM

High Quality, Affordable & Convenient Modern HMO Room

The cost of living is at an all-time high. Renting a room instead of a one-bedroom flat is becoming a practical and cost-saving option for many which has many, many benefits…..

Renting a room in a shared house could save you £500+ per month,

depending on your location. That’s money you could put towards savings, travel, entertaining or simply easing financial pressures.

Why Rent a Room in a modern new HMO?

  • Save Money – Lower rent and shared bills mean more financial freedom and no hassle or worries. 1 direct debit that’s it..
  • Community – Co-Living – Avoid Loneliness, build relationships
  • Fully Equipped – Fast Fibre WiFi broadband incl network points for Smart TV, Gaming, Desk to work from Home
  • Quality Kitchen & Appliances – Coffee machines, dishwashers, tumble dryers, no wet washing
  • Privacy – Ensuite facilities with decent showers / water pressure
  • Furnished – Bedroom furniture, storage, desk, dining area
  • Flexible Terms – Short and long-term rental options available.
  • Fully Managed Properties – Regularly maintained and cleaned for your comfort.
  • Great Locations – Rooms available in well-connected areas, close to amenities and transport links.
  • Move-In Ready – Furnished rooms with essential utilities included.
